What is sound and how is it made?

Sound is a type of energy made by vibrations that is created when something vibrates and sends waves of energy (vibration) into our ears.When the vibrations travel through the air or another medium (solid, liquid or gas) to the ear, we hear them as sounds.


In physics, sound is defined as vibrations that propagate as an acoustic wave, through a transmission medium such as a gas, liquid or solid. In human physiology and psychology, sound is the reception of such waves and their perception by the brain. -Wikipedia.
